FROM THE PRESIDENT'S DESK

Revered Parliamentarians

It gives us immense pleasure to invite you all to the inaugural edition of the Jhansi Youth Parliament scheduled this autumn, i.e., 30 September – 01 October, 2017 at Mahamaya School , Sector 44, Noida ,India.

Jhansi Youth Parliament will be a two day National Conference which seeks to bring together motivated and talented parliamentarians and provide a forum for meaningful debate on issues significant to National politics and Indian diplomacy.

The event calls upon parliamentarians from across the country to discuss about edge-cuttings and topics of national importance under one roof in committees like Uttar Pradesh Vidhan Sabha and National Security Council which are not commonly platformed under the YP umbrella. In the 2-day conference, parliamentarians will be assigned positions like ministers and members of parliament and shall utilise their full potential to seek the constituency they represent.

With a team of 30+ young hardworking individuals, the Secretariat is working really hard to provide you an experience to remember and memories to cherish for a lifetime. We strongly encourage you to take full advantage of this opportunity brought forward by Jhansi Youth Parliament.

Please do not hesitate to reach out to us with any questions or concerns. We look forward to welcoming you this autumn!
