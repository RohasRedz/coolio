Meet The Team

Rishabh Awasthi
Co- Founder & Co- President

Rishabh Awasthi is pursuing his Bachelors in Technology in Civil Engineering from Amity School Of Engineering, Amity University.He has been involved in MUN’s since high school . His key interests include indian politics, international relations and policy research. He has been the founder and Secretary General of St.Mary’s Model United Nations, an event which helped MUN’s to reach to the small towns in India and helped the students get to know about the International politics and diplomacy skills.He had been in the organizing committees as a secretariat member in various MUN’s and Youth Parliaments.Being a delegate,Rishabh has won the awards like Best Delegate, Best Speaker and others in many MUN’s and Youth Parliaments respectively.He has also served as an executive board member in MUN’s and Youth Parliaments.His last conference was Benaras Youth Parliament where he served as the Deputy Speaker for the Uttar Pradesh Vidhan Sabha

Rishabh has Trained school and college students for MUN’s, Youth parliaments and other literary activities.

Apart from MUN’s Rishabh is trained indian classical vocalist and guitarist and has represented his home state Uttar Pradesh at National Boxing Tournament.When free you can find him, listening to music or jamming with his guitar.
